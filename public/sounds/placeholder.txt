
This directory contains sound effects for the chess game:

- move.mp3: Sound for moving pieces
- capture.mp3: Sound for capturing pieces
- check.mp3: Sound for putting king in check
- checkmate.mp3: Sound for checkmate
- draw.mp3: Sound for stalemate or draw
- illegal.mp3: Sound for invalid moves
- promotion.mp3: Sound for pawn promotion

Please add MP3 files with these names to enable sound effects.
